VORAX INFESTATION VS BUILDINGS
All buildings destroyed on consumption. Creep replaces them. Fortifications → infested cover (benefits Vorax only). Shield Generators → energy absorbed by Hive Node. Reconquering + Creep fade (3-5 cycles) → rebuild from scratch. Total cost of losing a developed territory can exceed 50⚙ + 10 cycles.

Ground combat: 15% chance per building (5% if player wins). Orbital bombardment: ALL buildings destroyed. Sabotage: targeted single building. Destroyed buildings leave rubble (light cover, no function).

§16.4 — War Factory Functions

Auto-Production · Recruitment Unlock · Repair

AUTO-PRODUCTION
War Factory auto-produces 1 free unit every 2 turns. Unit type based on territory type + other buildings present. Adv. War Factory counts as 2× (produces faster or better units).
RECRUITMENT UNLOCK
Enables paid recruitment of heavy/vehicle units via RecruitPanel. Adv. War Factory additionally unlocks super-heavy/elite recruitment at the territory.
REPAIR / HEAL
Units at territories with War Factory heal between battles (restore HP). Damaged units automatically recover without spending additional resources.
Recruitment speed: No distance or factory-based speed modifier. A unit takes its build_time_cycles from the database. Period. No scrap cost scaling by distance. No build time scaling by distance.
Supply cut: Does NOT freeze recruitment. Supply cut only affects yield income (via supply chain hop modifier). Recruitment continues as long as territory is owned.
Damaged factory: No impairment from attacks. If you successfully defended the territory, the War Factory is fine.
§16.5 — Rally Point System

Per-System · Auto-Produced Units Gather Here

RALLY POINTS
Player designates a rally territory per system. All auto-produced units from War Factories in that system automatically gather at the rally point. This prevents units from scattering across 15+ territories in a system and lets the player concentrate forces at strategic locations.
BUILDING COST MODEL — ONE-TIME ONLY
All buildings have a one-time construction cost only. There is no ongoing upkeep. Once built, a building operates indefinitely until destroyed. This simplifies the economic model and makes territory development feel permanent and rewarding rather than a drain.
