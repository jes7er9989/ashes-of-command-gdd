THE EQUIPMENT SYSTEM
6 Slot Categories · 208 Items · 4 Rarity Tiers — Chapters 20-21
Every unit is a complete entity with its own identity. Customization comes from equipping weapons, armor, gear, ammunition, and modules. Equipment is acquired through research, battle loot, salvage, trade, and discovery. Higher-rank units unlock more slots.
The Six Slot Categories
Primary Weapon · Armor · Gear · Ammo · System · Special
[W] Primary Weapon
Main damage source. Found on all units.
[A] Armor Plating
HP bonus, damage resistance, special protection. All units.
[G] Gear
Tactical utility — grenades, scanners, repair kits. Infantry, Mechs. Locked at Rookie. Unlocks at Veteran.
[AM] Ammo Type
Modifies primary weapon damage type/behavior. Ranged, Vehicles, Ships.
[S] Synergy Module
Synergy equipment — targeting, sensors, ECM, engines. Vehicles, Air, Ships.
[SP] Special
Faction-unique or unit-unique signature equipment. Elite units only.

Rarity Tiers
Common · Uncommon · Rare · Legendary
COMMON
Available from start or early research. Baseline. No special effects.
UNCOMMON
Mid-game research or first loot/trade. Moderate bonuses. Minor effects.
RARE
Late-game research, specific encounters. Significant bonuses. Meaningful effects.
LEGENDARY
One-of-a-kind. Ancient Ruins, Guardian fragments. Cannot mass-produce. Game-changing.
Acquisition Channels
5 Ways to Get Equipment
Research (Tech Tree): Unlocks blueprints for mass production. Common + Uncommon.
Battle Loot (lootChance formula): Dropped by defeated enemies. Cross-faction possible. Uncommon + Rare.
Salvage: Post-battle scavenging (Horde is fastest). Common, occasionally Uncommon.
Trade (Black Market): Purchase with Scrap. Cross-faction items. Refreshes every 5 cycles. Uncommon + Rare.
Discovery: Ancient Ruins, Fallen Empires, Derelict Fleets. Rare + Legendary. The Keystone (+50% vs Guardians) = campaign-defining. One per campaign.

SHARED STRUCTURE
Branch 1: Primary weapon mastery → Ultimate weapon tech
Branch 2: Defense & sustainability → Ultimate survivability
Branch 3: Logistics & command → Ultimate strategic tech
Branch 4: Special projects → Super-heavy, flagship, hybrid ammo
TIER PROGRESSION
T1: 2 cycles. Common unlocks. Available immediately.
T2 (a/b choice): 4 cycles. Uncommon. Requires T1.
T3: 6 cycles. Rare. Requires T2.
T4 (Culmination): 8 cycles. Game-changing. Requires T3.
RESEARCH SPEED MODIFIERS
Research Lab: Enables research (1 researcher)
Advanced Lab: +50% speed (2 researchers)
Ancient Ruins trait: -25% time in that system
Architect secondary: +10% speed
CROSS-FACTION TECH ADOPTION
Defeat enemies → 15% loot drop → Use but not produce → Build Xenotech Analysis Bay (20⚙, 4 cycles) → Reverse-engineer (3-5 cycles) → Blueprint permanently available. Late-game luxury, not standard strategy.
