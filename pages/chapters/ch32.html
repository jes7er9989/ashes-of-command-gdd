The Five Endings
Alignment Determines the Galaxy's Fate
ARCHITECT: "The Ordered Galaxy"
Nexus imposes permanent cooperative resonance across all species. Galaxy is safe — but freedom is sacrificed to the system. 2-3 min cinematic if Architect is dominant alignment.
VANGUARD: "The Scorched Galaxy"
Nexus activated at maximum power. Vorax annihilated — but the galaxy is devastated in the process. Survival through total destruction. Shorter cinematic. Krell approves.
TYRANT: "The Iron Throne"
Commander seizes Nexus power. Vorax subjugated. Galaxy ruled as galactic deity. Order through dominance. The ending Vane has always believed was inevitable.
PYRRHIC VICTORY
Victory at catastrophic cost. The galaxy is saved — but your army is destroyed, your Generals dead, your civilization shattered. Rebuilt from nothing. Can trigger from any alignment path when losses exceed a threshold. Distinct cinematic — the weight of what was sacrificed.
UNIFIER (SECRET): "The Resonance"
Complete lore + complete alliances reverse the species-split. Galaxy healed. Vorax sealed permanently. Warp lanes reopened. The TRUE ending. Always the longest cinematic. Requires: balanced alignment + complete Unification negotiation + full Artifact chain. Full Guardian restoration. Reveals the Strategist’s true name. Valerius weeps.
