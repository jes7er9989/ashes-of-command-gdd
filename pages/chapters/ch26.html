Guardian Negotiation

Cannot Be Treated as a Standard Faction

COMMUNICATION REQUIREMENTS

Ancient Translator: Required item. Found via derelict or Fallen Empire Remnant exploration.
Signal Beacon: Build at 10⚙, 2 cycles. Deploy in outer rim system. Passive signal for 3 cycles before response.
POSSIBLE OUTCOMES
Wake-Up Call: Guardians confirm the simulation. Triggers Final War early.
Test: They attack. Hidden approval mechanic — how you fight determines what happens next.
Silence: No response. Attempt again in 5 cycles. Each attempt costs 1 Intel.
