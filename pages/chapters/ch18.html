Fleet Engagement Sequence
Pre-Battle → Formation Select → Auto-Battle → Resolution
Space battles trigger when fleets collide on warp lanes or when attacking a system. The player selects a formation in the Pre-Battle Window, then watches the auto-battle with CP intervention capability. Changing formation mid-battle costs 1 CP + 10-15 sec vulnerability.

§18.12 — MVP Armada PROTOTYPE LOCKED
2 Frigates · Persistent · No HP Restore Between Battles
Starting Fleet: 2 × Frigate (HP 120, Dmg 18, Atk Spd 1.0/sec, Move Spd 110 px/sec, Range 320 px).
Persistence: Armada persists for the full campaign run. Ships are NOT restored between battles.
Destruction: Destroyed ships are spliced from the array — permanently lost until replaced.
Replacement: New ships purchased via Armada Phase using Scrap. Cannot exceed fleet cap.
