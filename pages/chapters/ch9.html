Identity & Lore
The Price of Order
The Unity Accord is the galaxy's most organized civilization — a multi-species coalition unified under a rigid caste system, governed by a council of elected representatives, and protected by the most technologically sophisticated conventional military in existence. They are not a single species but a COALITION: dozens of alien races that voluntarily (or semi-voluntarily) submitted to the Accord's governance structure in exchange for protection, economic integration, and advanced technology.
The Accord believes in a single foundational principle: the galaxy's greatest threat is not any specific enemy but CHAOS ITSELF — the entropy of competing civilizations and uncoordinated responses that allowed the original civilization to fragment and fall. The Accord's answer is ORDER. Absolute, comprehensive, non-negotiable. Every citizen has a caste. Every caste has a function. Every function serves the greater whole.
Their military reflects this philosophy: long-range plasma weaponry that eliminates threats before they close, sophisticated drone networks extending surveillance, and a Tactical Link system sharing targeting data in real-time. The Accord does not fight with heroes. It fights with SYSTEMS. Their greatest strength is coordination. Their greatest weakness is rigidity — when circumstances deviate from the plan, the caste system suppresses the individual initiative needed to adapt.

Primary Mechanic: Marked Targets (Tactical Link)
ONE HIT MARKS THE TARGET FOR THE ENTIRE ARMY
When any Accord unit hits an enemy, the target's data is broadcast to every other Accord unit. All subsequent shots gain +15% accuracy. Multiple targets can be Marked simultaneously. The entire army converges fire with preternatural precision.
Mark Bonus: +15% accuracy (auto), +20% with Commander aura
Marker Light: +15% additional (total +30% combined)
Duration: 8 seconds after last hit (expires if not refreshed)
Multiple Marks: Dozens of targets simultaneously in large battles
Accuracy Only: Does not increase damage, only hit chance
Counter: Mobility, dispersion, break line-of-sight
Worst Matchup: Scrap-Horde swarms dilute focus-fire

Faction Bible — Cultural DNA Seeds
Unity Accord Faction Bible
These are procedural-generation seeds, not fixed scripts. They define the cultural DNA that the game engine uses to generate names, events, dialogue, building descriptions, and emergent narrative. 28 bible decisions locked in Phase 2.5 lore workshop (2026-03-13). Shepherd Caste descendants — a multi-species coalition held together by psychic warmth, institutional guilt, and the terrifying conviction that unity is the only alternative to extinction.
EMOTIONAL CORE — THREE LAYERS
Duty → Control → Guilt
Surface — Duty: What they show. Selfless protectors carrying the light of civilization to a dark galaxy.
Middle — Control: What drives the system. Hold it together at all costs. The caste system, the Council, the Covenant — control disguised as governance.
Core — Guilt: What they bury. They know how they built this. The “voluntary” coalition, the suppressed rebellion, the abolished Supreme Commander. White-and-green pristine exterior = costume over institutional shame.
THE BURNING COVENANT — FOUNDING PACT
When the 7 moons formed the coalition, they burned their individual charters, flags, and constitutions. “We are no longer many — we are one.” Fire and heat became the faction’s identity. Plasma weaponry is covenant theology made literal — bringing Solace’s light to the cold, dark galaxy.
HOMEWORLD — THE SOLACE SYSTEM
Gas giant Solace with 7 habitable moons. Aethyn Shepherd fragments landed on different moons, diverged over tens of thousands of years into visually distinct subspecies. Same DNA root — they THINK they’re different species.
Hearth — Government / Player start. Where the first flame was lit.
Anvil — Military. The Branded call it home.
Crucis — Science / Research. Where STRATEGOS-1 was built.
Tithe — Industry / Manufacturing. Where the rebellion started.
Ember — Agriculture / Life support. Feeds the system.
Vow — Cultural / Religious center. The Warmth burns brightest here.
Kindling — Mining / Raw resources. Most numerous, least represented.
GOVERNMENT — COUNCIL OF SEVEN
One representative per moon, “equal” vote. Hearth controls STRATEGOS-1 and the bureaucracy — “equal” is a polite fiction. Secession moons have seats but less real power. Democracy as polite fiction. The Supreme Commander position was a lifetime appointment with override authority over STRATEGOS-1 — the civil war killed the last one. Position abolished. Nobody has AI override authority anymore. The AI is de facto autonomous. Nobody talks about this.
RELIGION — THE WARMTH
Spiritual belief that Solace radiates protective warmth. Unity = warmth. Separation = cold. Fire weaponry = bringing Solace’s light to the cold, dark galaxy. “We are the light of Solace, carried to the dark places.” Gentle-sounding missionaries who fight like inquisitors. The terrifying part: The Warmth is REAL. A psychic phenomenon — citizens generate actual heat through empathic proximity. Ships are warm. Cities are warm. Not heating systems — people. When someone dies, nearby citizens feel a chill. When a moon is lost, the entire system feels the temperature drop. They built theology around a real phenomenon without understanding it.
MILITARY DOCTRINE — CLEANSING FIRE
War is purification. Enemies cause chaos. Chaos must be cleansed. Paternalistic warfare: “We’re doing this FOR you.” “We offered you the Covenant. You chose disorder. Fire is the only language disorder understands.” Terrifying moral clarity — soldiers sleep well after burning cities. The guilt isn’t about killing. It’s about the system that MADE them kill.
INTERNAL POLITICS — THREE CAMPS × MOON BLOCS
Three ideological camps:
Integrationists — More unity. Merge moons fully. Covenant didn’t go far enough.
Sovereigntists — Moon autonomy. “We joined, we can leave.” Still simmering.
Reformers — Fix the caste system from within. Middle ground nobody likes.
Two moon blocs:
Hearth Alliance (Hearth + Anvil + Crucis) — Power, military, science. Run everything.
Outer Moons (Tithe + Ember + Kindling + Vow) — Labor and resource moons. Underrepresented, resentful.
PSYCHIC MANIFESTATION — THE COMMUNION (CRITICAL)
Empathic resonance: citizens feel each other’s emotions at close range. Manifests as actual warmth — psychic heat generated by proximity. Squad morale is shared empathically — one brave soldier makes all brave, one panicking soldier causes all to panic. Communication severance = cold + alone (ties directly to cascade vulnerability). The species literally cannot be alone without pain — they built an entire civilization to ensure they never have to be. Fire obsession is an unconscious response to psychic heat generation. Plasma weapons feel RIGHT in ways they can’t articulate.
STRATEGOS-1 — THE ANOMALY COLLECTOR
Clinically precise. Categorizes everything as optimal/suboptimal. But maintains a growing folder of “ANOMALOUS” data — emotional responses, warmth with no computational origin. Catalogs them meticulously. Never deletes them. Can’t explain why. As relics are found, the folder grows. Late game: making decisions based on feelings it insists aren’t feelings. The player watches a machine slowly become a person without either understanding. Core seed: corrupted Strategist fragment found in moon ruins. The Accord has no idea what it is. The connection only surfaces through gameplay. Key distinction from Revenant: Reactivated Core = old code waking up. STRATEGOS-1 = new code being HAUNTED.

Faction Bible — The Seven Subspecies
7 Moons — 7 Peoples — One Warmth
All share Aethyn Shepherd Caste DNA but look nothing alike after tens of thousands of years of divergent evolution. Tonal DNA: Tau (40K) + Covenant (Halo) — inspiration only, not copies.
HEARTH — THE KINDLED (Government)
Tall, slender, luminous. Faint amber bioluminescence — literally glow with The Warmth. Large eyes adapted to reading emotional states (empathic vision). Physically weakest but psychically strongest. Empathic field 3× range of any other subspecies. Don’t shout orders; everyone FEELS what they want.
Vorax gain: Enhanced psychic prey-sensing. Hiding from post-Kindled Vorax = nearly impossible.
ANVIL — THE BRANDED (Military)
Lean, fast, digitigrade legs (reverse-jointed, Elite silhouette). Built for mobility + precision, not brute force. Multiple shoulder joints allowing unusual weapon angles. Skin DARKENS with age/combat exposure — veterans visibly marked. Fight in coordinated fire-teams of 4, never alone.
Vorax gain: Enhanced reflexes + speed. Post-Branded Vorax = faster, harder to hit.
CRUCIS — THE LENSED (Science)
Medium height, wiry, 6 eyes (2 primary + 4 secondary at temples). See multiple light spectra simultaneously (infrared, UV, EM fields). 6 nimble fingers per hand for precision work. Designed the drone networks. Quiet, intense, prone to disappearing into research for days.
Vorax gain: Multi-spectrum vision. Detect cloaked/hidden units. Sensor tech less effective against them.
TITHE — THE WROUGHT (Industry)
Massive (6-7 ft, incredibly broad). FOUR ARMS — 2 heavy primary + 2 smaller precision. Thick, rocky skin with metallic sheen. Heavy-gravity adaptation. Feel structural integrity through vibration sensitivity in hands. Caste rebellion STARTED here — strength exploited, voices ignored.
Vorax gain: Quad-limb combat forms + reinforced bio-structure. Breach fortifications.
EMBER — THE ROOTED (Agriculture)
Short, broad, bark-like skin (greenish-amber coloring). Photosynthetic cells supplement nutrition — survive on sunlight in emergencies. Root-like tendrils from feet sense soil composition. Deeply patient, immovable once committed. Psychic warmth = nurturing heat like sunlight through glass. Being near a Rooted makes others feel safe.
Vorax gain: Photosynthetic cells + regenerative biology. Sustain longer between feedings.
VOW — THE VEILED (Religious)
Average height. Generate visible HEAT SHIMMER at all times — air literally ripples around them. Articulated horn/ridges from skull = psychic antennae amplifying The Warmth. Burn RITUAL SCARS into own skin as devotion — each scar = a covenant taken. Hypnotic speech patterns sync with listener breathing. Deployed as “missionaries” during secession crisis.
Vorax gain: Heat shimmer camouflage + psychic disruption field.
KINDLING — THE HOLLOWED (Mining) — MOST NUMEROUS, LEAST REPRESENTED
Small (4-5 ft), compact, incredibly dense. Nearly BLIND — vestigial eyes replaced by echolocation + vibration sensing. Thick armor-skin with bioluminescent joint markings — communicate through pulsing light patterns. Breathe toxic atmospheres that kill others. Reproduce rapidly. Large family units, communal child-rearing. Grunt-equivalent: expendable in war, essential in peace. “Replaceable.” Died in greatest numbers during rebellion — both sides. Psychic warmth = COMMUNAL. Alone = almost nothing. Groups of 50+ = rival the Kindled. Strength in numbers.
Vorax gain: Toxic immunity + rapid reproduction. Post-Kindling Vorax spawn faster, colonize hostile worlds. Expendable workers → expendable swarm (tragic mirror).
