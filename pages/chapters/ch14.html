Static Hazard Zones (3-6 per galaxy, permanent)

Permanent Features That Shape Strategy

Nebula
2-3 system span
Fleet stealth inside (invisible to outside sensors). Shields disabled for all. Sensors -50%. Ambush zone — kinetic fleets hiding inside dominate energy-shielded fleets.
Black Hole
1-system radius
No retreat within 1 hop (fight to destruction). No salvage (wreckage pulled in). Time dilation: +1 cycle research time. Commitment zone — you either win or die.
Asteroid Field
Band/cluster
+1 cycle movement through field. Fighters +30% evasion (dodge between rocks). Capitals 2% HP/cycle collision. +25% Scrap production. Light fleet defense zone.
Pulsar
Rotating beams
EMP every 3 cycles — disables tech modules 1 cycle in adjacent systems. Comms +1 cycle delay. 1% HP radiation. Horde least affected (crude tech). Accord most affected (electronic targeting). Megastructures (Ring Worlds, Dyson Spheres, Aethyn Nexus) may interact with galactic phenomena — a Dyson Sphere in a Pulsar’s EMP range could have its energy output disrupted. See Ch. 13 for megastructure profiles.
Wormhole Zone
Spatial distortion
Contains wormhole endpoints (instant transit shortcuts). 5%/cycle fleet displacement (wrong destination). 20% false sensor contacts (ghost fleets). High-risk, high-reward navigation.
Dynamic Events (temporary, 1 cycle warning)

The Galaxy’s Weather — Unpredictable, Impactful

This chapter covers galactic-level phenomena — events that affect star systems, warp lanes, and fleet operations. For planetary-level weather that affects ground combat (sandstorms, blizzards, eruptions), see Ch. 13.

Nebula Surge (every 8-12 cy, 3 cy duration)
Nebula EXPANDS +1 adjacent system. Shields stripped. Fleets gain stealth. Exploit: position fleet in predicted path for sudden ambush capability.
Gravitational Pulse (every 10-15 cy, instant)
Black Hole PULLS all fleets within 2 hops 1 hop closer. Involuntary. Can drag fleets into no-retreat zone. Keep 3+ hops away or accept the risk.
Meteor Shower (every 6-10 cy, permanent deposit)
6-12⚙ Scrap deposited on random territory near Asteroid Field. Land-grab race between factions. "Are we really fighting over 10 Scrap?" Sometimes yes.
Pulsar Flare (every 12-18 cy, 1 cy duration)
ALL electronics disabled in adjacent system for 1 full cycle. No combat, no movement, no comms. Fleets are sitting ducks. Time assaults to coincide.
Wormhole Resonance (every 15-20 cy, 3 cy duration)
SECOND temporary wormhole opens connecting to random distant system. Instant transit but 40% collapse chance per use (vs 5% standard). 3 cycles then closes. An irresistible gamble for emergency reinforcement or surprise attack.
ION STORMS — MOBILE HAZARDS
Spawn every 8-12 cycles at galaxy edge. Travel inward along warp lanes at 1 hop/2 cycles. Duration per system: 2-3 cycles. Effects: ALL shields disabled, ALL tech modules offline, ALL electronic equipment non-functional, random 1-3% HP ion lightning.

Terran: Lose targeting but kinetics still fire. Field Repair drones go OFFLINE.
Shards: Phase Blink unaffected (not electronic).
Horde: LEAST affected (crude non-electronic tech).
Revenant: Gauss weapons require power — reduced to basic mode.
Accord: Tactical Link OFFLINE — devastating.
