STARVATION SYSTEM
Cycle 1-2: Warning. Emergency reserves consumed.
Cycle 3-4: Mild. -10% combat effectiveness. No re-equip.
Cycle 5-6: Moderate. -25% effectiveness. 1% HP/cycle attrition.
Cycle 7+: Severe. -50% effectiveness. 2% HP/cycle. 5% desertion chance/cycle.
Scrap-Horde: 50% starvation resistance (all timelines doubled). Necro-Legion: immune to biological starvation (equipment degradation only — no HP attrition or desertion).

§15.9 — Canonical Stat Application Order PROTOTYPE LOCKED
The Sequence That Resolves All Modifier Conflicts
base stats → equipment mods → supply penalty → space combat ground mod → rank mods
① BASE
Unit archetype stats. HP, Dmg, Atk Spd, Move Spd, Range.
② EQUIPMENT
W/A/G/AM/S/SP slot bonuses applied multiplicatively.
③ SUPPLY
Attacker or Defender penalty (see §15.7–15.8) applied to result.
④ SPACE MOD
spaceCombatResult groundDamageMod (0.80/1.0/1.15) applied to damage only.
⑤ RANK
Rookie +0% · Veteran +5% · Elite +12% · Commander +20%. Applied last.
