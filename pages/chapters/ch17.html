THE AUTO-BATTLE ENGINE
Core Engine · AI Roles · Space Combat · Ground Cover — Chapters 17-19
All combat runs on a single auto-battle engine. The player does NOT directly control units — they fight autonomously according to AI behavior scaling with veterancy rank. The player's role: pre-battle preparation (80% of tactical skill), real-time CP ability intervention, and strategic observation for future decisions.
The Auto-Battle Principle
Preparation → Observation → Intervention
PRE-BATTLE (80%)
Equipment loadouts. Army composition. Deployment positioning (ground) or formation selection (space). A well-prepared army wins before the first shot.
REAL-TIME INTERVENTION
CP abilities: orbital strikes, shield projections, reinforcement drops, retreat orders. Pauses battle when activated. The commander's surgical tools.
STRATEGIC OBSERVATION
Which units underperform. Which compositions are problematic. Which terrain is underutilized. Feeds into replay coaching for General-led battles.
ENGINE ARCHITECTURE — SIMULTANEOUS SYSTEMS
Pathfinding: Navigate terrain, avoid obstacles, seek optimal positions based on AI behavior.
Targeting: Priority rules — nearest, weakest, highest threat, commander designation.
Combat Resolution: Fire rate → accuracy roll (range, cover, Marks, Phase Blink) → damage (weapon type, armor, resistances).
Morale: Bio units track morale. Low morale = accuracy penalties, slower movement, routing. Machines/energy immune.
Special Mechanics: Field Repairs, Phase Blink, Wreckage Salvage, Reanimation, Marked Targets, Energy Absorption, Volatile Death.
Faction Overlays: Each mechanic runs as an overlay on core engine — consistent base, unique per faction.

Ground Combat — Quick Reference
Cover · Elevation · Garrison
COVER SYSTEM
Light Cover: -15% damage (vegetation, debris, low walls)
Heavy Cover: -30% damage (buildings, thick walls, wrecks)
Garrison: -40% damage + suppression immune (interior)
DESTRUCTIBLE: All cover has HP. Sustained fire removes it.
ELEVATION
+1 Tier: +10% acc · +5% range · +5% dmg
+2 Tier: +15% acc · +10% range · +10% dmg
+3 Tier: +20% acc · +15% range · +15% dmg
Deployment: High ground = most impactful pre-battle choice.
GARRISON
-40% damage + suppression immune
Fire from windows with normal accuracy
Cannot move until voluntary exit
Building destroyed: 80% HP damage to all occupants.
