Espionage Operations
Five Operation Types
Recon1 IntelReveals fleet composition, garrisons, buildings, planetary traits. Persists 5 cycles. Foundation for all other ops.
Deep Recon3 IntelRequires existing Recon. Reveals EXACT equipment loadouts, named Commanders + traits, victory conditions, hidden installations. The Approach Phase Espionage output.
Sabotage4 IntelTargets specific building for destruction. 60% base success (Sensor Spires −15% each). Failed = spy detected/captured, reputation penalty.
Counter-Intelligence2 IntelDeploy to friendly system. Persistent. 40%/cycle detection chance. Detected spies expelled (return in 2 cycles). Cunning Generals +20% detection.
Extraction3 IntelRescue captured spy. 40% success. Failed = BOTH agents lost (original executed). High-risk, high-reward.
ESPIONAGE VS VORAX
Standard ops don't work. Special ops:
Synapse Tracking (2I): Pre-battle map of Synapse positions.
Adaptation Analysis (3I): Reveals current mutation resistances + optimal weapon mix.
ESPIONAGE VS GUARDIANS
Standard ops don't work. Intel redirected to research:
Lore Fragment Discovery (4I): Cross-reference data to synthesize Guardian lore fragments. 1 per op. Repeatable every 5 cycles.

BETRAYAL CONSEQUENCES
Betrayed faction: −50 rep, decays +3/cycle, won't talk for 10 cycles. · Other factions: −20 rep, decays +2/cycle, demand higher Influence costs. · Your Generals: Loyal trait loses −5 loyalty. Cunning unaffected. Ambitious gains +2 (approves pragmatism).
Economy
Three Resources · Trade Routes · Black Market · Pirates
⚙ SCRAP
Primary resource. Buildings, units, equipment, fleets, terraforming. Generated by territories, Scrap Mines, Trade Hubs, Wreckage Salvage.
◈ INTEL
Intelligence resource. Espionage ops, research acceleration, Deep Recon. Generated by Sensor Spires, espionage networks, territory control.
⬡ INFLUENCE
Diplomatic resource. Audiences, alliances, Unification path milestones. From Embassies, Memorials, territory. Accord gets +50% bonus.

TRADE ROUTES
Trade Hub pairs connected by warp lanes generate +3⚙/cycle each. Disrupted by Ion Storms, blockades, or Vorax consumption of intervening systems. No inflation — costs static. Challenge comes from threat scaling.
PIRATES & BLACK MARKET
NPC pirates in 1-2 systems. Raid trade routes (−20% income). Options: destroy, bribe (10 cycles), or hire as mercenaries. Black Market sells cross-faction equipment, refreshes every 5 cycles. Cunning General defectors become Pirate Warlords.
