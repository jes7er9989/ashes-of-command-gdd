THE GALACTIC COMPENDIUM
500-800 Entries · Three Voices · Persistent — Chapter 34
A LIVING DOCUMENT. Personal record of everything discovered, fought, and survived. Fills as you play. Three voices: Commander Notes (subjective, sometimes wrong — the player's own lens on events), AI Data Entries (clinical, precise, cross-referenced), Advisor Commentary (marginal notes per doctrine — Valerius, Krell, and Vane annotating YOUR records).
