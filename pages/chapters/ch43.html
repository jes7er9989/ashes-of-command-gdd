Planned DLC Expansions
Three Major DLCs + Conceptual Future Content
The Swarm UnboundDLC 1
Playable Vorax. Bioevolution War Room replaces equipment (mutate organisms between battles). Mutation Strain tree: Speed/Armor/Adaptation branches (choices lock in). Campaign: consume enough Biomass to birth a Queen-Ship that breaches the Core Guardian exclusion zone. 5 new evolutionary unit branches.
The Core AwakensDLC 2
Playable Core Guardians. Start with maxed tech, "Tall" 2-3 system empire. Global Observation (entire map visible from turn one). Directive Interpretation: Strict (destroy all biological life) vs Adaptive (protect galaxy from Vorax by any means). The inverse question: "What do I do now that I AM the Core?"
The Schism WarsDLC 3
Prequel campaign. Play AS the Original Strategist during the species-split. Schism Points mechanic: decisions push factions toward independence or unity. Reveals complete hidden history. Retroactively recontextualizes ALL base game Artifacts on replay — the reason for the DLC's place in the release order.
Conceptual Future ContentDLC 4+
"The Pirate Kings" — Playable raiding-economy faction · "The Last Alliance" — 2-5 player cooperative vs Vorax + Guardians · "Endless Crucible" — Roguelike mode with daily seeds and escalating modifier stacks · New faction concepts: interdimensional traders, rogue AI collective, time-displaced human colony.
