Identity & Lore

The Long Goodbye

The Eternal Shards are the last surviving fragment of the galaxy's most ancient civilization — a species that mastered psychic manipulation, energy-matter conversion, and dimensional folding when the other factions' ancestors were still single-celled organisms floating in primordial seas. They are not warriors by nature. They are scholars, seers, and artists who were forced to become warriors when the universe proved indifferent to cultural achievement.
They are dying. Not quickly — not in a single cataclysm — but slowly, generationally, as their population dwindles, their Mnemo-Cryst infrastructure crumbles — memory-matter losing coherence, millennia of archived thought dissolving into psychic static — and their psychic talents weaken with each successive generation. Every Eternal Shard alive knows they may be among the last of their kind. Every battle they fight costs lives they cannot replace. Every unit lost is a unique consciousness — a mind trained for centuries in psychic disciplines — that vanishes forever from the galaxy's psychic fabric. Every structure that falls is a library of ancestral memory shattered beyond recovery.
Their strategy reflects their desperation: they field small numbers of extraordinarily powerful elite units, shielded by rechargeable energy barriers and capable of briefly flickering out of reality to dodge incoming fire. They do not swarm. They do not grind. They strike with devastating precision and withdraw before the enemy can bring numbers to bear. Every engagement is a calculated risk — they can win any battle, but they cannot afford the casualties of victory.
Visual Identity

Crystalline Psychic Elegance

Ground Forces
Mnemo-Cryst armor — memory-matter, a semi-organic crystalline lattice grown from the Shards' own thoughts as psychic scaffolding. Older veterans wear denser armor, physically heavier with centuries of archived memory woven into the crystal matrix. Shimmers with iridescent colors shifting with light angle. Weapons PROJECTED from the user's psychic field — hard-light blades from forearms, crystalline rifle constructs forming around hands. Troops glide rather than march, movements unnaturally graceful.
Air Forces
Grav-craft hanging without visible propulsion, anti-gravity fields distorting light beneath. Swept, organic, transparent airframes — pilot visible inside a crystal canopy growing from the fuselage like a jewel. Weapons fire as beams of prismatic light refracting into rainbow spectra.
Space Fleet
Mnemo-Cryst ships glowing with internal psychic energy — translucent hulls dense with millennia of archived memories, crew silhouettes faintly visible like shadows in amber. Capital ships are physically heavier than escorts: not from thicker plating, but from deeper memory. Breaking a hull doesn't just destroy a vessel — it shatters a library of ancestral thought. Solar sails of woven light. Move silently without chemical thrust, propelled by psychic impulse. In formation: a constellation coming alive.
Gameplay

Primary Mechanic: Phase Blink (Reality Refraction)

UNITS FLICKER OUT OF REALITY TO DODGE PROJECTILES
Every Eternal Shards unit has a base chance per incoming projectile to trigger a Phase Blink. They do not dodge. They do not deploy physical shields. Through a micro-fold in local spacetime, the unit forces reality to briefly rearrange itself so that the incoming projectile was never going to hit them in the first place. To an observer, the Shard simply flickers out of existence for a microsecond and reappears two meters away, untouched. It is automatic, passive, and mathematically infuriating to fight against.

Faction Bible — Phase 2 Lore

Eternal Shards Faction Bible

These are procedural-generation seeds, not fixed scripts. They define the cultural DNA that the game engine uses to generate names, events, dialogue, building descriptions, and emergent narrative. 16 decisions locked in Phase 2.2 lore workshop (2026-03-13).
EMOTIONAL CORE
Harmony (Desperate) — “Connection is everything.”
The echo of Aethyn unity. Every psychic bond is a fragment of what was. They seek connection — with each other, with the cosmos, with the signal they can almost hear. The faction that comes CLOSEST to remembering what it felt like to be Aethyn. But do not mistake this for serenity. The Communion is not peaceful — it is loud, chaotic, and saturated with the grief of a dying species. Every mind in the network feels the population counter ticking down. They cling to the bond not out of cultural preference but because severance from the Communion is not isolation — it is a literal, agonizing psychological death. They are not serene. They are drowning, and the Communion is the only thing keeping their heads above water. They are The Dying Light. Tyrant corruption: Forced connection. “You WILL join us. Resistance is a disease.”
GOVERNMENT
Council of Resonances. Specialized psychic roles: precognitives handle strategy, empaths handle diplomacy, telekinetics handle military, crystal-shapers handle infrastructure. Organized, functional, but siloed — turf wars between specializations dressed in crystal robes. Each council believes their talent is the key to survival.
HOMEWORLD — SHAEL
A verdant world where nature and crystal grew together — organic forests intertwined with crystal formations. FRACTURED by the founding relic crisis — the planet literally broke. Healed with psychic-crystal growth. Visible fault lines sealed by crystal bridges. A broken world that chose to heal itself through connection. Parallel to Terra: same Aethyn relic trigger, different response (political unity vs. physical healing).
FOUNDING CRISIS — THE GREAT COMMUNION
The Aethyn relic triggered a planet-shattering crisis. One powerful psychic heard the resonance first — instinctively understood crystal healing. Chain reaction pulled every mind on the planet into a single shared experience. For one moment, they were Aethyn again — one mind holding the world together. The hidden truth: The Great Communion was an echo of Aethyn psychic unity triggered by a Strategist fragment. The first psychic who heard it was the first being since the Fracture to hear the Strategist’s signal. The Council of Resonances was founded to preserve that structure. They worship the moment they accidentally remembered what they were.
RELIGION — ANCESTOR REVERENCE
They sense echoes in the psychic field — dim impressions interpreted as ancestors speaking. Actually detecting Aethyn psychic residue embedded in the crystal matrix. The closest faction to the truth: their “ancestors” ARE the Aethyn. They have no idea how right they are. Aligns with Phase 1.10: “Crystallized ancestors. Echoes of our true form.”
SOCIAL STRUCTURE — COMMUNION + SPECIALIZATION
Base layer: Psychic communion. Emotions bleed. Memories shared. Isolation = punishment. Being alone is not solitude, it’s death — a literal, screaming psychological unraveling that no Shard has ever survived with their mind intact. The network itself is not a calm pool of shared thought; it is a torrent of collective grief, desperate hope, and the constant low-frequency hum of a species counting its own extinction in real time. Professional layer: Specializations create work identity. You FEEL with everyone, WORK with your type. Tension: What you feel in communion vs. what your role demands. An empath soldier who feels the enemy dying. Their elegance on the battlefield is not grace — it is the hyper-efficient panic of a species that cannot afford a single casualty. Every perfect maneuver is born from the terror of irreplaceable loss.
ECONOMY — CRYSTAL COMMUNION (SEED)
Crystal is the base of everything — currency, building material, psychic amplifier, and living infrastructure. Crystal-shapers are the economic backbone. Resources flow through psychic consensus rather than markets. Need is communicated through the communion; supply is directed by the Council. Scarcity is felt by everyone simultaneously. (Economy model to be formally locked in Phase 3.)
XENOPHOBIA — LAYERED BY SPECIALIZATION
Empaths: Pity — “they’re deaf to the universe.” Precognitives: Fascination — “how do they function without foresight?” Telekinetics: Unease — “the silence around them is disturbing.” Each council sees non-psychics through their own lens. Consensus: non-psychic species are incomplete, pitiable, but potentially valuable.
NAMING CONVENTIONS
Formal: Psychic-tonal resonance names (vowel-heavy, flowing, carry psychic frequency). Saying a name correctly creates micro-resonance. Outsiders butcher them. Common: Shortened for daily use and non-psychics. The real name is the psychic one — the common name is the compromise. Examples: Vey’shael’oran → Veysha. Thae’lumis’ent → Thaelen.
MILITARY DOCTRINE — HYBRID GLASS CANNON
Layer 1 — Precognitive Command: See the future, position forces before the enemy knows where they’ll attack.
Layer 2 — Psychic Assault: Break minds before bodies. Demoralize, confuse, terrify. Physical units mop up.
Layer 3 — Crystal Infrastructure: Seed battlefields with amplifier crystals. Longer they hold, stronger they get.
Failure mode: When one layer fails, others cascade. Jammed precog = blind assaults amplified by crystals pointing the wrong way. Complexity is strength AND vulnerability.
TECH AESTHETIC — LAYERED (RAW → REFINED → LIVING)
New structures: Raw crystal growth — jagged, organic, still forming.
Established: Shaped by psychic intent — precise, beautiful, impossibly elegant.
Ancient/critical: Semi-sentient. Walls thicken under attack. Rooms reconfigure. Tech reads psychic input.
Ships (exception): 100,000+ year Aethyn relics. Not built — maintained. Translucent Mnemo-Cryst hulls, crew silhouettes visible like shadows in amber. The oldest ships are the densest — their crystal lattice compressed under the weight of a hundred thousand years of accumulated memory. Impossibly ancient, impossibly perfect.
INTERNAL FACTIONS ON SHAEL — SPECIALIZATION SCHISMS
After the Great Communion faded, specializations claimed territory. Precognitives think they should lead. Empaths think they should heal. Telekinetics think they should protect. Crystal-shapers think they should build. Unity broke along talent lines. Commander must prove harmony > specialization — the whole is greater than parts. Mirrors the Aethyn: castes split into factions, now specializations split into factions. The pattern repeats at every scale.
FIRST CONTACT DOCTRINE
Player choice moment — advisors disagree, Commander decides. The psychic dimension changes everything: they FEEL the other faction before seeing them. Preserver: “Listen first. Understand what they are.” Shield: “Defensive formation. Protect the communion.” Weaver: “Reach out. Touch their minds. Learn everything.”

Faction Bible — AI Director Seeds

Procedural Generation DNA

VORAX ADAPTATION — CRYSTAL CORRUPTION
No biomass to consume — Shards biology is crystalline. Vorax absorb Mnemo-Cryst material instead, and what they gain is far worse than armor. The twisted crystalline growths sprouting from Vorax carapaces are not just decoration — they are hijacked broadcast towers. The consumed Mnemo-Cryst retains its psychic resonance, but the Vorax corrupt the frequency. Instead of harmony, the crystalline growths broadcast agonizing psychic static back into the Shards’ Communion — a screeching, corrupted echo of ancestral memory turned into noise. Fighting the Vorax is not just a physical battle for the Shards; it is deafening psychological torture. Every Vorax unit wearing stolen crystal is a migraine that screams in the voice of the dead. Sacred material made grotesque. The most visually horrifying adaptation — the enemy wears your ancestors.
NEGOTIATION AT THE NEXUS
Recognition — “We’ve Been Listening.” The entire religion prepared them for the Warden-Commander without knowing it. Meeting the Guardian = puzzle piece clicking. Not worship — understanding. “Oh. It’s YOU.” The most authentic faction-Guardian encounter in the game. They were right all along.
CASCADE VULNERABILITY — PSYCHIC + CRYSTAL
Psychic Disruption: Ion storms, Fracture flares, Vorax biofields → communion static → councils fragment → scared individuals.
Crystal Destruction: Physical damage → amplifiers dark → abilities drop to baseline → glass cannon without the cannon.
DIVERGENCE — SINGLE AXIS (PURITY ↔ SURVIVAL)
The ONLY question for a dying species: “How do we survive?” Purity: Extinction with dignity. Die as Shards. Survival: Alive but transformed. Something new. Expressions vary by alliances, enemies, what they adopt. Most emotionally devastating divergence — no winning, just choosing what you lose.
