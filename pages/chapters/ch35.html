EMPIRE DELEGATION
Sector Command · Fleet Command · Coaching · Override — Chapter 35
SECTOR COMMAND
General governs 2-4 systems. ALL military ops within sector run autonomously. 9 standing orders available. Personality modifies order interpretation. Auto-resolves battles. Player receives tiered After-Action Reports (summary → detailed → full replay).
FLEET COMMAND
General commands mobile Armada. Moves and fights independently on galactic map. Personality determines order interpretation. Aggressive DEFEND = aggressive counter-offensive. Reckless ESCORT = may abandon convoy to chase targets. Player must account for trait when assigning.
REPLAY COACHING — 5 TAGS MAX
Review General's auto-resolved battle replays. Tag up to 5 non-conflicting priorities: FLANK MORE · HOLD POSITION · PROTECT TRANSPORTS · FOCUS HVTs · CONSERVE FORCES · USE COVER · PRESS ADVANTAGE · RETREAT EARLIER. Tags persist and modify General AI behavior in future engagements.
CATASTROPHIC OVERRIDE
Generals handle their own problems UNLESS catastrophic: losing entire sector, Vorax breakthrough, General loyalty crisis. Catastrophic events trigger a non-blocking notification. General handles immediate response while player completes their current activity without interruption.
