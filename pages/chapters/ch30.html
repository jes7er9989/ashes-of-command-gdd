VETERAN XP & UNIT PROGRESSION
Kill-Count Promotion · Commander Rescue · Slot Unlocks — Chapter 30
Units grow through kills, not battle counts. Harder battles yield more XP. 10 brutal sieges may promote faster than 25 easy skirmishes. Every named unit is an investment — their loss is felt strategically and narratively.

Commander Rescue Rules
Unit Destroyed — Two Possible Fates
BATTLE WON
Unit counts as wounded. Removed from field. Returns after 3 cycle recovery. All XP, traits, and equipment preserved. Terran bonus: Field Repair may prevent reaching 0 HP.
BATTLE LOST
Unit is captured or killed — fate determined by captor. Necro-Legion: interrogated, then reanimated as enemy. Vorax: consumed and assimilated. Guardians: vaporized on contact. Named units lost permanently = narrative event.
