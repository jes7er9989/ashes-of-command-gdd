META-PROGRESSION & THE ARCHIVE
Three Layers · Simulation Data · Account Persistence — Chapters 33-34
Each simulation run generates a fresh galaxy. Death resets In-Run progress — but strategic knowledge, blueprints, and accumulated resources persist across runs through a multi-layered Archive. A third Account layer persists across entire campaigns, rewarding veteran players permanently.
The Three Layers

In-Run · Archive · Account

LAYER 1: IN-RUN
RESETS EACH SIMULATION RUN
Territory control, resources, unit veterancy, equipment inventory, research progress, faction relationships, Commander rank. The standard strategy game loop — build, fight, grow, potentially lose everything.
LAYER 2: THE ARCHIVE
PERSISTS ACROSS SIMULATION RUNS
Simulation Data (SD): Meta-currency earned from completed runs
Blueprint Unlocks: Researched Common/Uncommon equipment permanently available
Starting Bonuses: +5⚙ Scrap, +1 Rookie per SD upgrade (stacking)
Commander Perks: Permanent HP, CP regen, aura radius upgrades
Lore Fragments: All discoveries persist in Compendium across campaigns
LAYER 3: ACCOUNT
PERSISTS ACROSS ENTIRE CAMPAIGNS
Meta Modifiers: +2% XP, +1⚙ Scrap per completed campaign (stacking, uncapped)
Commander Titles: "The Unifier," "The Conqueror," "The Survivor," faction-specific
Legacy Bonuses: +2% per faction per campaign (max +10% per faction)
Legacy Echoes: Legendary Generals appear as recruitable mercenaries in future campaigns
New Game+ Modifiers: Iron Galaxy · Accelerated Vorax · Generous Core · Fog of War+

Empire Delegation (Ch 35)

The Galactic Emperor Shift — General Autonomous Command

SECTOR ASSIGNMENT
General governs 2-4 systems. Controls ALL military operations within. Standing orders modified by personality. Auto-resolves battles — player receives tiered After-Action Reports (summary → detail → full replay).
FLEET ASSIGNMENT
General commands a mobile Armada. Moves and fights independently on the galactic map. Personality modifies order interpretation: Aggressive on DEFEND = counter-offensive. Reckless on ESCORT = may abandon convoy to chase targets.
REPLAY COACHING (Max 5 Tags)
Review General's auto-resolved battle replays. Tag up to 5 non-conflicting priority instructions: FLANK MORE · HOLD POSITION · PROTECT TRANSPORTS · FOCUS HVTs · CONSERVE FORCES · USE COVER · PRESS ADVANTAGE · RETREAT EARLIER. Tags modify General AI for future engagements.
CATASTROPHIC OVERRIDE
Generals handle their own problems UNLESS catastrophic: losing entire sector, Vorax breakthrough, loyalty crisis. Catastrophic events trigger notification but do NOT pull the player from current activity. General handles immediate response while player finishes.
